Come Stand Up Men (DT2)

Come stand up men and sing for Dartmouth.
Cheer when the team in green appears.
For naught avails the strength of Harvard,
When they hear our mighty cheers!

Fight, fight, fight for Dartmouth,
And tear on down the field.
Touchdown, Touchdown, Dartmouth,
For the old Crimson's strength must yield.
