Join the Band

The DCMB welcomes new members from any grade and at any time of the year, and there are no auditions. You can even borrow an instrument if you don't have one, and we'll get you set up with a uniform and music as fast as can. We'll have a number of events for freshmen during Dimensions and Orientation. If you're already here, just come to one of our rehearsals. If you have more questions, you can contact our publicity chairs, Tyler Spunaugle and Maggie Flanagan. Either way, it's even easier for us if you fill out the following form to let us know you're interested.
